LUMI course¶

Get the data:

wget https://a3s.fi/gmx-lumi/aqp-240122.tar.gz
tar -xf aqp-240122.tar.gz
cp aquaporin/topol.tpr .

gmx_mpi mdrun -g ex1.1 -nsteps -1 -maxh 0.017 -resethway -notunepme
